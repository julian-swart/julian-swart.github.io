Who Am I?

My name is Julian Swart and I hold a Master’s in Business Analytics from the University of Tennessee. I started this website to show my enthusiam for statistics and computing. I enjoy improving upon my craft of problem solving and programming. More generally, I am interested in math, philosophy, and psychology.

I am currently employed as a remote Data Scientist at Vans, a VF Company. Before that, I was a Data Strategist at Pilot Company in Knoxville, TN. For both jobs, I have focused on Consumer Insights, specifically centered around Loyalty and Marketing.

I live in Wilmington, NC and my hobbies include surfing, fishing, guitar, photography, kamado joe cooking, exercising, reading, and listening to audiobooks/podcasts.
