JJ Furniture - Part 1

Julian Swart Dec 2022 3 min read


Instead of using an online dataset, a colleague and I decided to simulate what a real company’s data looks like - since this is what we use in our day jobs (but are unable to share since it’s their data). We came up with a fake business called JJ Furnitiure (JJ stands for Jon and Julian) and created artifical transaction, customer, and product data.

This collaboration project stemmed from wanting to deepen our understanding of the entire data process and needing to showcase some of our capabilities to the public. Part 1 will display what we learned about data engineering. Part 2 will exhibit some of our data science skills.

def get_ids(table_name):
    
    query = '''
    select {}_id
    from {}
    '''
    
    results = db_conn.queryall(query.format(table_name[:-1], table_name))
    
    return results[results.columns[0]].values.tolist()

    select cost
