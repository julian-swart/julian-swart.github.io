Fake Business

Jonathan Adams and Julian Swart Dec 2022 1 min read


Data can be separated into two stages: processing it for use (data engineering) and using it (data science, analytics, ML, etc.). At work, my colleague and I largely stay within the scope of the latter. That simultaneosly changed for both of us when the less popular but immensely impactful subject of data engineering suddenly became the focus. This collaboration project stemmed from wanting to deepen our understanding of the entire data process and needing to showcase some of our capabilities to the public. Part 1 will display what we learned about data engineering. Part 2 will exhibit some of our data science skills.

Instead of using an online dataset, we decided to simulate what a real company’s data looks like - since this is what we use in our day jobs (but are unable to share since it’s their data). We came up with a fake business called JJ Furnitiure (JJ stands for Jon and Julian) and created artifical transaction, customer, and product data.
